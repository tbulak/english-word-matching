# Word Matching Game

## Overview
The Word Matching Game is an engaging and educational game designed to improve vocabulary and cognitive skills. Players are tasked with matching words with their corresponding meanings, synonyms, or related words in a fun and interactive way.

## Features
- **Multiple difficulty levels**: Choose from easy, medium, or hard levels to match your skill.
- **Timer**: Race against the clock to complete matches.
- **High Scores**: Track your best scores and compare them with friends.
- **Custom Word Lists**: Add your own words and definitions to create personalized challenges.

## How to Play
1. Start the game by selecting a difficulty level.
2. The game will present you with a set of words and definitions or related words to match.
3. Drag and drop the words to their corresponding matches or click to select the pairs.
4. Complete all matches before the timer runs out to earn points.
5. Review your matches after the game ends, and see your high score!

## How to Add Custom Words
To add custom words to the game:
1. Open the `custom-words.js` file located in the project directory.
2. Add your words in the following format:
   ```javascript
   const customWords = [
     { word: 'example', definition: 'a representative form or pattern' },
     { word: 'match', definition: 'to bring two things into harmony or agreement' }
   ];
   ```
3. Save the file and restart the game to include your new words.

## Conclusion
Enjoy playing the Word Matching Game and enhance your vocabulary through fun challenges! Explore the possibility of adding your own words and customize your gaming experience.
